Storage Size Guide

5x5 Storage Unit

A 5x5 storage unit, measuring 5 feet in width and 5 feet in length, offers about 25 square feet of space with varying heights, typically around 8 feet. This size is equivalent to a large closet and can store a variety of items, including but not limited to:

  • Boxes of Personal Items: Ideal for storing several small to medium boxes containing personal items, clothes, documents, books, and other small household items.
  • Furniture: Small pieces of furniture such as a dresser, a small mattress set (e.g., twin size), a nightstand, a small desk, chairs, and dismantled furniture parts.
  • Seasonal Items: Seasonal decorations, sports equipment, and clothing can be stored off-season to free up space at home.
  • Electronics and Appliances: Small appliances, TVs, computers, or stereo systems.
  • Office Supplies: Small business owners or those working from home can store office supplies, small file cabinets, books, and documents.
  • College Dorm Contents: Suitable for storing the contents of a college dorm room during the summer months or a gap year, including a mini-fridge, microwave, books, and clothes.
  • Hobby and Sporting Equipment: Bicycles, golf clubs, camping gear, fishing gear, or craft supplies.

It's important to organize the storage unit efficiently to maximize space. Use shelving units if allowed, stack boxes safely, and place items you may need access to near the front. Ensure that sensitive items are properly protected, considering climate control if the items are sensitive to temperature or humidity.

10x10 Storage Unit

A 10x10 storage unit provides 100 square feet of space with a typical height of about 8 feet. This size is equivalent to an average-sized bedroom and can accommodate a substantial amount of items, making it suitable for a wide range of storage needs, including:

  • Household Furniture: A 10x10 unit can store the contents of a one- or two-bedroom apartment, including sofas, a dining set, bedroom sets (queen or king-size bed), dressers, coffee tables, and chairs.
  • Appliances: Large appliances such as refrigerators, washers, dryers, stoves, and dishwashers can fit in this space, ideal for those in the process of moving or renovating.
  • Boxes and Containers: Ample room for stacking numerous boxes and containers, making it perfect for storing seasonal items, clothes, books, documents, kitchenware, and other household items.
  • Office Equipment: Suitable for businesses looking to store desks, chairs, filing cabinets, computers, printers, and boxes of documents or inventory.
  • Garage Contents: Can accommodate tools, lawn equipment, small tractors, bicycles, motorcycles, and sports gear.
  • Vehicles: Depending on the height and door size of the unit, a 10x10 space may fit smaller vehicles or motorcycles, along with their accessories.
  • Large Collections or Hobbies: Ideal for storing large collections, such as comic books, vinyl records, or art supplies, as well as hobby-related items like crafting materials or large musical instruments.
  • Business Inventory: Provides ample space for small business owners to store inventory, promotional materials, and seasonal stock.

A 10x10 unit is versatile, offering enough space for significant personal, household, or business storage needs. It's an excellent option for those looking to store the contents of a small apartment, excess business inventory, or large items during a move or renovation.

10x15 Storage Unit

A 10x15 storage unit provides 150 square feet of space, typically with an 8-foot ceiling, making it comparable to a large bedroom or a one-car garage. This size can accommodate a more extensive range of items, ideal for storing the contents of a multi-bedroom house, larger items, or comprehensive needs such as:

  • Full Household Furniture: Enough space for furniture from a 2-3 bedroom house, including multiple large furniture pieces like sofas, beds (including king-sized), dining sets, dressers, large appliances, and several large boxes.
  • Appliances: Can store several large appliances at once, such as refrigerators, washers and dryers, ovens, and air conditioning units, making it suitable for renovations or moves.
  • Office Equipment and Files: Ample room for office furniture, including desks, chairs, filing cabinets, conference tables, and boxes of documents, making it a good choice for businesses during relocation or for document archiving.
  • Vehicle Storage: Some 10x15 units are large enough to store vehicles such as cars, small boats, or multiple motorcycles, along with related gear and tools.
  • Workshop Space: Can be used as a workshop area for hobbies that require space, such as woodworking, large scale painting, or sculpture.
  • Business Inventory: Offers sufficient space for small to medium-sized business owners to store inventory, equipment, or promotional materials.
  • Seasonal and Recreational Items: Ideal for storing seasonal decorations, sports equipment, camping gear, and recreational vehicles like ATVs or snowmobiles during the off-season.
  • Large Collections and Memorabilia: Provides a climate-controlled environment (if chosen) for large collections of books, comics, vinyl records, antiques, or memorabilia.

A 10x15 unit is versatile enough to serve various storage needs, from comprehensive household storage during moves or renovations to business inventory and equipment storage.

20x20 Storage Unit

A 20x20 storage unit offers 400 square feet of space, with a typical height of about 8 feet. This size is equivalent to a standard two-car garage and can accommodate a wide array of items, making it suitable for extensive storage needs such as:

  • Entire Household Contents: Can store the contents of a 4-5 bedroom house, including large furniture (sofas, beds, dining sets, dressers), household appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers), and numerous boxes.
  • Large Appliances and Furniture: Ample space for storing large appliances, oversized furniture, and specialty items like pianos, large sculptures, or pool tables.
  • Vehicles: Suitable for storing cars, boats, trailers, or several motorcycles, with room left for spare parts, tools, and maintenance equipment.
  • Business and Commercial Inventory: Ideal for businesses requiring space for inventory, equipment, large machinery, or archives of documents. Also suitable for storing office furniture during relocations or renovations.
  • Workshop or Studio Space: Provides enough room to set up a workshop for hobbies that require significant space, such as woodworking, metalworking, large-scale art projects, or band rehearsals.
  • Seasonal and Recreational Equipment: Can accommodate seasonal items, including holiday decorations, lawn and garden equipment, sports gear, camping equipment, and recreational vehicles like ATVs or snowmobiles.
  • Extensive Collections and Archives: Offers a climate-controlled environment (if chosen) for protecting large collections of books, comics, vinyl records, antiques, or memorabilia from environmental damage.
  • Construction Materials and Tools: Suitable for contractors or renovators to store large quantities of materials, tools, and equipment needed for construction projects.

A 20x20 storage unit is exceptionally versatile, catering to both personal and business storage needs on a large scale. Whether for storing the contents of a large household, a vehicle collection, or business inventory, this size unit offers ample space to meet a broad range of storage requirements.
