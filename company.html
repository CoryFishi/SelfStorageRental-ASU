About Us

DISCOVER STORAGERENT

StorageRent emerges as a prominent leader in the U.S. self-storage sector. Established in 2002, our journey has significantly influenced the storage market landscape. Over the recent five years, we've expanded our national footprint with an impressive addition of $274 million in new acquisitions.

At the heart of StorageRent's operations are our valuable relationships with stakeholders, partners, investors, and customers. Our ambitious objectives are powered by a committed and innovative team, dedicated to revolutionizing the storage industry.

CUSTOMERS

You've likely spotted a StorageRent sign close to where you live or work. Our reach extends across 34 states and Washington, D.C., boasting ownership or management of over 3,000 facilities, encompassing roughly 1.5 million units and 28 million square feet of rentable space.

At StorageRent, we prioritize security, appealing landscaping, and top-tier property management for each location we acquire, construct, or oversee. A StorageRent facility integrates seamlessly into your community, supported by our dedicated on-site managers, staff, and valued customers.

Room for everything you love most!

Our customers are navigating life's transitions, and StorageRent is here to support you through each significant change. The items you store with us are integral to your future's promise, and we take our responsibility to safeguard your possessions very seriously.

We recognize that our customers demand a secure, clean environment coupled with straightforward, effective service. Our commitment is to not just meet, but exceed these expectations daily. Our team is crucial in upholding this standard of excellence throughout every phase of our customer's journey with StorageRent.

INVESTORS

Secure Your Future with StorageRent Investments

StorageRent Inc. (NYSE: SR) stands as a fully integrated, self-administered, and self-managed real estate investment trust (REIT), proudly part of the S&P 500. Our investment philosophy is straightforward: enhance shareholder value through focused growth and delivering outstanding performance. Our team is committed to this goal, demonstrating consistent excellence in implementing our strategic initiatives. These initiatives include expanding our market presence, boosting facility occupancy rates, enhancing our third-party management services, and increasing our digital footprint.

Our approach to growth and meticulous execution has significantly elevated shareholder value, setting us apart from our competitors in the self-storage sector. Over the last decade, StorageRent's total return to shareholders not only leads the self-storage REIT category but also ranks at the forefront among all publicly traded REITs, irrespective of property type.
