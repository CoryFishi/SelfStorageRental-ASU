Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I choose the right size storage unit?

A: Consider the items you plan to store and how much space they will occupy. Our website offers a size guide with recommendations based on common storage needs, such as furniture, boxes, and appliances. For personalized advice, contact our customer service team.

Q: What are your hours of operation?

A: Our facilities are accessible from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, including weekends and holidays. The office is staffed Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

Q: Can I access my storage unit outside of normal hours?

A: Special after-hours access can be arranged in certain circumstances. Please discuss your needs with our office staff to find a solution.

Q: What do I need to rent a storage unit?

A: You will need a government-issued photo ID, the first month's rent, a security deposit, and a completed rental agreement. We accept payments via credit card, check, or cash.

Q: Is there a minimum rental period?

A: The minimum rental period is one month. After the first month, the rental is month-to-month with no long-term commitment required.

Q: How do I terminate my rental agreement?

A: We require written notice 30 days before your intended move-out date. Notice can be provided via email or in person at our office.

Q: How much does it cost to rent a storage unit?

A: Prices vary based on the size of the unit and location of the facility. Please visit our pricing page or contact our office for the most current rates and special offers.

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

A: We accept credit cards, debit cards, checks, and cash. You can also set up automatic payments for convenience.

Q: Are there any additional fees?

A: A one-time administrative fee is required at the time of rental. Insurance coverage is also required, which you can provide through your insurer or purchase from us.

Q: How secure are your storage facilities?

A: Our facilities are equipped with 24-hour surveillance cameras, secure fencing, and individual alarms on each storage unit. Access is controlled through a personalized security code.

Q: Do I need insurance for my stored items?

A: Yes, insurance is required for all stored items. You may use your own insurance policy or purchase insurance from us at a reasonable rate.

Q: What items are prohibited in storage units?

A: Prohibited items include perishables, flammable liquids, explosives, toxic materials, and illegal substances. If you're unsure whether an item is allowed, please contact our office.

Q: Can I store a vehicle in my storage unit?

A: Yes, we offer specific units designed for vehicle storage, including cars, motorcycles, and small boats. Please ensure the vehicle is in working condition and provide proof of ownership and insurance.

Q: Who is responsible for the maintenance of the storage unit?

A: We maintain the cleanliness and security of common areas. Tenants are responsible for keeping their units clean and reporting any maintenance issues promptly.

Q: Can I change to a different size unit if I need more or less space?

A: Yes, you can upgrade or downsize your unit based on availability. Please contact our office to discuss your needs and arrange for a transfer.
